## âš™ï¸ Troubleshooting

### Problem: Can't Login
**Solutions:**
- Check your email and password
- Ensure you have internet connection
- Verify you are registered as a "Reader"
- Contact administrator if issue persists

### Problem: Can't Download Schedules
**Solutions:**
- Make sure you're logged in
- Check internet connection
- Verify schedules have been assigned to you
- Contact administrator to assign schedules

### Problem: Can't Submit Readings
**Solutions:**
- Check internet connection
- Ensure all required fields are filled
- Verify meter reading is valid (not less than previous reading)
- Try again later if server is busy

### Problem: Token Expired
**Solution:**
- You'll see: "Token expired. Please login again."
- Simply login again with your credentials
- Your offline readings will be preserved
